Vikranth: A Mixed Reality Gaming Experience

Duration: two weeks

You step into a thrilling mixed reality quest where your room transforms into battlefield. Your goal? Find and shoot enemies while dodging bullets and dangers. As a seasoned treasure hunter nearing retirement, you'll use your wits to uncover these weapons scattered among your room space. You'll strategize your moves to overcome obstacles and stay safe in this immersive experience that blurs reality and virtual worlds.

Ideation Phase

The primary goal is to shoot the alien/runic species while dodging their bullets and get the treasure. Time is running fast, your quick thinking will change your outcome.

Key Highlights

Immersive Gameplay -- Environmental Awareness -- Immersive Spatial Audio -- Haptic and Audio Feedback -- Scan and Define Play Area -- Replicability


Storyboarding

Target Audience

It appeals to tech-savvy gamers aged 18-26, offering immersive MR experiences. Players navigate ancient battlefields, seeking hidden weapons amidst traps, blending strategy and adventure. Its narrative depth attracts adults seeking unique entertainment, while fps (First Person Shooter) find the theme intriguing.

AGE RANGE: 18-36

core Loops

The player scans his environment and using controllers to move around and shoot the enemies using trigger buttons with help of the gun.The player's score increases with each enemy being destroyed. Meeting specific milestones or reaching a certain score threshold unlocks rewards with every level.

process review

Installation of XR Interaction Toolkit:

We installed XR Interaction Toolkit in Unity to enable VR Interactions.

Installation of AR Foundation:

We configured necessary settings in Unity for AR experiences.

Asset Selection from Storyboard:

Assets from the storyboard were selected upon and chosen further for the game.

VR Intro Creation:

Using Unityâ€™s Asset Store and blender, the cave scene was created.

Asset Acquisition and Modification:

Obtain bots and torpedo samples from Unity Asset Store and modify them according to project needs.

Treasure Chest Modelling in Blender:

Treasure chest was modelled in Blender and then exported as a .FBX file.

Game Script Creation:

Game script was developed to facilitate interactions and gameplay mechanics.

Implementation of Sound Design:

Narration - AI text to speech software (Resemble AI) Ambient sound for cave. Special effects - gun sound - blast sound effect (Pixabay).

First Prototype Implementation:

  • Implement basic gameplay elements:
  • Implement grabbing functionality.
  • Spawn enemies within the environment.
  • Establish kill zones with necessary colliders.
  • Integrate scoring system and timer.
  • Develop a game controller with three states. (Start, Waiting and Over)

Second Prototype Implementation:

  • Game Play mechanics and features were further improved.
  • Implemented the gun and the scoreboard.
  • Added the treasure chest and the final level.
  • Implemented the game over screen.
  • Implement haptic feedback for enhanced user experience.
  • Improve movement and direction of the enemies for a more realistic behavior.
  • Utilize Scoreboard on the gun to view scoreboard in a natural way.
  • Enhance lighting within the environment.
  • Adding spatial UI for better accessibility.

Iterative Testing and Refinement:

Conducted user testing to gather feedback and improve on the game.


Future Enhancements:

  • Introduce multiplayer features for shared exploration experiences.
  • Implement a wider range of weapons and power-ups for more engaging gameplay.
  • Enhance the environment with more interactive elements and obstacles.
  • Implement AI challenges that dynamically adjust based on user performance, ensuring an optimal level of difficulty.
