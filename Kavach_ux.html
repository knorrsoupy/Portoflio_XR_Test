KAVACH UX: A Privacy focused App

Duration: three weeks
Team Members: 6

Amidst a data-rich era, the fate of our digital assets remains uncertain. This project delves into the posthumous transfer of data and digital assets, exploring the destiny of personal information while aiming to create a smooth transition process and address the data's status.

OUR GOAL

Our goal is to set up an effective, ethical, humane and secure inheritance system for the digital assets we want to pass on to our successors and educate the masses about the same.
